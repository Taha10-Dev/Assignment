About Us

The KSIJ – CBE pledges to recognize and embrace each student’s uniqueness and construct a solid bridge that facilitates the transition from child to young adult by providing a challenging curriculum that encompasses the intellectual social and physical needs of our children. The CBE will promote a positive environment that nurtures excellence in teaching and learning resulting in high academic achievement for all students.

The Khoja Shia Ithnaasheri Jamaat (KSIJ) is a nonprofit organization providing holistic and affordable Education services through the Al Muntazir schools, to 5,500 students of various ages and cultural backgrounds from Toddlers age to Secondary A Levels. In 2013, the KSIJ CBE established a Special Needs Unit, whereby different abled children are trained under capable staff.

The KSIJ CBE employs more than 570 professional and non-professional staff to ensure the Schools delivers cost effective education and are preferred education providers in the country. With a history dating back to 50 years, the Al Muntazir Schools have rich traditions and strong professional accomplishments. Our oldest member, the Union Nursery School celebrated its 60th Anniversary in 2017. In the same year, the Al Muntazir Islamic Seminary accomplished 35 years of its journey towards providing quality education in the field of Secondary Education at Form 1 to Form 4, offering Cambridge IGCSE/GCE Curriculum. The A Level Curriculum is offering CAMBRIDGE Curriculum.

VISION

The vision of KSIJ CBE is to provide exemplary education to diverse community at Al Muntazir Schools where students are prepared to excel in a diverse interconnected global environment and contribute to make this world a better place.

MISION

The KSIJ – CBE pledges to recognize and embrace each student’s uniqueness and construct a solid bridge that facilitates the transition from child to young adult by providing a challenging curriculum that encompasses the intellectual social and physical needs of our children. The CBE will promote a positive environment that nurtures excellence in teaching and learning resulting in high academic achievement for all students.
